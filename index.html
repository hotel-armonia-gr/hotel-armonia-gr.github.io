The Armonia Hotel is a family and traditional island hotel. It belongs to the 3-star category of Santorini hotels and operates from the 20th of April to the 20th of October. Located in the beautiful village of Kamari in the southeast of the island, the Armonia hotel is the perfect vantage point from which you can enjoy tranquil and pleasant vacations near the sea.

The management and personnel of the Armonia hotel, take great pride in its organized facilities and efficient hotel services, as well as in its 30 fully-equipped and tastefully-designed rooms and studios for accommodation in Santorini Greece.

Enjoy Santorini in an environment of exceptional aesthetic appeal and warm and friendly atmosphere.
