Services & Facilities

The hospitable management and personnel of the scenic hotel Armonia, offers you a variety of well organized hotel facilities and services, providing both comfort and affordable prices during your stay at the unique placement of Armonia hotel.
Armonia hotel includes the following facilities:
Reception desk (operating: 07:00 - 23:00)
A swimming pool
A pool bar
Main lobby
Breakfast Room
TV Room
If you are planning to make your vacation in Greece and to the unique island of Santorini, Armonia hotel provides you a comfortable and relaxing accommodation to its rooms. In advance, you have the opportunity to choose through a various types of accommodation the one that is the most suitable for you. The different types of hotel accommodation of Armonia hotel (rooms and studios) offer spacious places in the units and are elegantly designed, all in comforts to insure the guests of our hotel for a pleasant stay. We are eager welcome you to Armonia Hotel in Santorini and to the marvelous village of Kamari!
Armonia hotel also offers the following Services:
Daily main service
Fax transmittal
Car & motorbike rental service
Travel information
Wireless Internet access
Wake-up services
Safe deposit box
Luggage storage at the main lobby
Communal bathroom
